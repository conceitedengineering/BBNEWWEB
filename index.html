About
‍
BWP is an art & design collective focusing on print and the web. We seek to work on projects that are driven by collaboration and community.
‍
We foster community by working on educational zines and offering our print services to movement organizers and students.

We nurture our collaborative spirit by working on virtual art shows that are not desperately nostalgic of physical space.
‍
We hope to continue being a resource and platform for others while evolving our own archival and visual practices.
 
Based in the San Francisco Bay Area, on unceded Ohlone land.
‍
Contact us for services, ideas, collabos and advice on your projects.
